Create Potions and Chords

Directions:

  • Drag the ingredients from the column on the left into the cauldron on the right.
  • Click on an ingredient to hear its pitch.
  • The row above the cauldron tells you which ingredients have already been added.
  • Click the reset button to start over.
  • Click the play button to hear the chord created by your potion.
  • Click the mix button to create your potion and recieve your potion's score.
